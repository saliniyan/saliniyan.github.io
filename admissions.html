Admissions

Admission Requirements

At Erode Government School, we welcome students from diverse backgrounds. To apply for admission, please ensure you have the following:

  • Completed application form
  • Copies of academic transcripts
  • Birth certificate
  • Parent/guardian contact information
Admission Process

Our admission process includes:

  1. Submit your application form
  2. Attend an interview (if required)
  3. Receive admission decision
  4. Complete enrollment
Important Dates

Application Deadline: August 31, 2024

Interview Dates: September 10-15, 2024

Enrollment Deadline: September 30, 2024
